Nordea

Nordea is the largest financial services group in the Nordic region and one of the biggest banks in Europe. With design direction by Bold Scandinavia we have developed the new Nordea Sans typeface. All in all 18 styles, divided in 2 subfamilies for Text and Display. Nordea Sans Small is optimized for body copy and Nordea Sans Large, with more personality is fitted for headlines.
